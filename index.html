Features

Farmer App

An all-in-one solution for every farm or shop set-up. Farmers are the beating heart of their businesses, let Growing Green be the brain.

Key Features

  • Offline field logs with voice notes and GPS
  • Inventory, harvest, and employee management
  • Local marketplace for direct sales
  • Worker punch in/out with sync
  • Communicates with both the Market App and Worker app to give you full control over your business.

New Features Coming soon!

Market App

A full farmer's market in your pocket. Shoppers find local farms and shops to purchase the freshest foods and unique experiences at the best prices. Seamless and painless.

Key Features

  • Geolocate small farms and businesses within a 0-300 mile radius
  • Subscribe to CSAs, buy small, buy bulk, set appointments.
  • Make purchases in-app, pick up, or get delivered

Why Our Pricing?

Growing Green is built to reduce overhead and boost efficiency for small farms and entrepreneurs. The subscription covers unlimited access to management tools, offline sync, and AI features (coming soon), while royalties ensure we only succeed when you do—aligning our goals with yours.

Lower Than Competitors

Comparable farm management software like Farmbrite ($59/month) or SafetyCulture (~$50/month per user) often costs 25-50% more. We offer similar or better features at a fraction of the price, making advanced agtech accessible without breaking the bank.

Tax Deductible

For U.S. businesses, software subscriptions like the Farmer App are fully deductible as ordinary business expenses. Royalties on sales may also qualify as deductible costs—consult your tax advisor for details.
